Charlie Blackwood, the love interest for the character played by Tom Cruise in the 1986 film “Top Gun,” may be a fictional character, but she was inspired by a real CNA analyst: Christine Fox.

Goose

Lieutenant (junior grade) Nick "Goose" Bradshaw was a naval flight officer and LT Pete "Maverick" Mitchell's radar intercept officer (RIO).

Iceman

Tom "Iceman" Kazansky is one of only a few United States naval aviators to be sent to TOPGUN. He is known for his big ego, as is his RIO, LTJG Ron "Slider" Kerner. His signature bleached hair tips are closely associated with the fact that he is "the best pilot" in the new class of TOPGUN students.

Details
Viper

Commander Mike "Viper " Metcalf is a naval aviator and an instructor at TOPGUN. Viper is an old friend of LT Pete "Maverick" Mitchell's father Duke Mitchell.

Details
Jester

An ace fighter pilot, Jester serves along side Mike "Viper" Metcalf as an instructor at the Top Gun school. As he explaines to the students at the Top Gun, the school's job is to make sure that the students knew how to effectively use their fighter jets to accomplish their missions and survive.
